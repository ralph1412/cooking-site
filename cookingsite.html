The White Sauce Pasta

The following steps are the steps for cooking perfect and delicious Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of boiled pasta
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoon of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon crushed garlic
  • 2 tablespoon of butter
  • 2 teaspoon chili flakes
  • salt as required
  • 1 tablespoon pf crushed black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 cup grated packaged mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup chopped veggies[bell peppers,onion,olives]
    • Step 1: In a pan heat the oil and butter. Add the all-purpose flour to it and mix well until it becomes a thick paste.
    • Step 2: stir fry the veggies in seperate pan with some salt and black pepper.
    • Step 3: Add the milk to the paste, and stir well. Add the chili flakes, crushed black pepper, and salt. Now add the fried veggies. Mix well
    • Step 4: When the sauce seems ready add the boiled pasta in it. Mix it well.
    • Step 5: Once the pasta seems to be mixed well with the sauce add the grated cheese and leave it to melt, do not stir the pasta.

    Enjoy!
